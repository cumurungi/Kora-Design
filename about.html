About us

Kora Design's mission

A curated interior design matchmaking platform, built for homeowners who want a refined experience and measurable outcomes.

Kora Design was founded to close the gap between homeowners and talented designers. We vet each designer by portfolio, process, and user satisfaction, and we create intentional matches based on style, budget, and project scope.

Our philosophy is simple: great design begins with excellent listening, strong visuals, and an easy path from inspiration to installation.

Our story

From a passion for design to a platform that connects visionaries.

Founded in 2026, Kora Design emerged from the founders' own frustrations with the disjointed interior design process. After years of working in architecture and design, we saw a need for a more streamlined, trustworthy way for homeowners to find the right designer.

Today, we serve hundreds of clients across the US, from urban apartments to suburban homes, ensuring every project reflects the client's unique style and lifestyle.
